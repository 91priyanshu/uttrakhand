SEMINAR HOUSE

SEMINAR HOUSE

This bright and welcoming seminar room of 65 sqm with its wide arched windows offers space for yoga, meditation, seminars, bodywork and workshops in a relaxed holiday atmosphere. It has beautiful views onto the lush green palm tree garden. The room has air conditioning to keep it cool in summer, and there is heating and a lovely fireplace in addition for the wintertime.

FACILITIES

A large variety of seminar facilities are available :

Chairs, Meditation Cushions, Backjacks
Yoga Mats, Mattresses , Candles,
Blankets, Tibetan bowls
Hifi sound system, Microphones
Beamer,Screen,Wifi
Flipchart, White Board

SPACES

The place has a deep silence and tranquil energy and is perfect for yoga and meditation retreats and workshops for personal development. Enjoy meeting or your exercise in the spacious seminar room and in the beautiful outside areas in midst of the tropical garden or pool area.

EATING

We offer delicious vegetarian food served in the beautiful atmosphere of our terrace or the dining area. If you like to cook yourself or bring your favourite cook, you can rent our kitchen for self-catering. Facilities include a stove and oven, refrigerator, cooking equipment, crockery and cutlery. There is also a romantic barbecue area or you can enjoy the traditional local restaurants and cafes at the beach, or in Denia.

IMPRESSIONS

Our goal is to provide a place that is more than just a guesthouse. A place where guests can feel an immediate connection to beauty and nature.
