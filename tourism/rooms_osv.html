ROOMS

BEAUTIFUL ROOMS

From high-class to low-budget rooms, Take your pick of a cosy dormitory, Or a grand room overlooking the palm garden

You can book single or double room, equipped with stylish furniture and highspeed wifi access. Our rooms are bright and ornate, with lovely colourful floor tiles and a unique design in each.

EL GRECO – Double room Superior

With elegant furniture, an antique Spanish design, lovely paintings, beautifully tiled floors and an ensuite bathroom this splendid room has the touch of an aristocratic bedroom. Large windows flood the room with light, and there is a patio living room overlooking the palm garden. Enjoy the A/C, the use of a fridge and free wifi access. A single bed can be added.




GOYA -Double room Superior

This elegant room has an antique Spanish design, with classical paintings and lovely wood-panelled walls. It is complete with a large ensuite bathroom and a beautiful patio living room overlooking the lush garden, a lovely spot to relax and unwind. Enjoy the A/C, the use of a fridge and free wifi access.




PICASSO – Twin room Standard

This room is a cosy, colorful space decorated with fine art paintings. There is a private bathroom outside the room. Enjoy the A/C, the use of a fridge and free wifi access.




DALI -Double room Standard

The stylish, light-flooded, modern room with a large panoramic window and ensuite bathroom is enchanting with its view of the blue sky, green palm trees and the picturesque courtyard with a fountain. The beautiful patio living room offers a breathtaking view of the famous Montgó mountain, and a lovely spot to relax and unwind. Enjoy the use of free wifi access.




APARTMENT Superior (max.5 persons)

This family size apartment is also lovely for a couple who value space. It is on the second floor of the house, with gorgeous sweeping views out of wide arched glass windows. From the beautiful loggia you can look out onto the lush palm garden below, and onto the nearby mountain and even a distant view of the Mediterranean. 2 spacious bedrooms, a bathroom, a kitchen with: gas cooker / fridge / microwave / ironing board / washing machine and dining room surround a lovely central living area and extra bed space in the large sofa. Big Fan towers available. Enjoy the free wifi access.




MIRÓ -Double room Standard

A modern double room with private bathroom and private terrace. The bright walls are decorated with artful flower paintings and the floor has unique tiles. Big windows offer views of Montgó mountain as well as our palm tree garden. The terrace is a perfect spot to unwind and enjoy the afternoon sun. The room has a fridge and free wifi access.




Our goal is to provide a place that is more than just a guesthouse. A place where guests can feel an immediate connection to beauty and nature.
